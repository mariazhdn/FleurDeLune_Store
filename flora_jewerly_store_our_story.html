The Story of Fleur De Lune

The Origins

Born From the Moon and Flowers

The Fleur de Lune story began in the heart of Paris, where a young jeweler, inspired by the cool glow of the moon and the delicacy of night-time flowers, opened his first private atelier.

The brand's core idea was to immortalize the fleeting beauty of nature in precious metals. The first collections gained recognition thanks to the complex technique of invisible stone setting and the use of the rarest emerald and lunar raw materials.

The Modern Era

A New Paradigm of High Jewelry

Today, the House of Fleur de Lune combines the centuries-old jewelry traditions of French masters with cutting-edge 21st-century design.

Each new piece is a manifesto of absolute luxury, created for those who value individuality and impeccable artistic style. We remain true to our roots, carefully passing on the secrets of our craft from generation to generation.
